1. Introduction

Welcome to Kanhero! This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our Kanban board application. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our practices.

2. Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information.

Kanhero is designed with privacy in mind:

  • All your boards and cards are stored locally on your device
  • We do not have servers that store your data
  • We cannot access, view, or retrieve your tasks
  • No account or registration is required

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored using Android's local database system (Room/SQLite):

  • Board names and settings
  • Card content and status
  • App preferences (theme, sounds, haptics)

This data remains on your device and is never transmitted to external servers.

4. Third-Party Services

Kanhero uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Play Billing: Used solely for processing in-app purchases (unlimited boards upgrade). Google's privacy policy applies to payment processing.

We do not use any analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

5. Permissions

Kanhero requests the following permissions:

  • Vibration: For haptic feedback when interacting with cards (can be disabled in settings)
  • Internet: Required only for Google Play Billing verification

6. Data Backup

Android Auto Backup may back up your app data to Google Drive if enabled on your device. This is controlled by your Android settings and Google's privacy policy.

7. Children's Privacy

Kanhero does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The app is designed for general audiences and does not contain any age-restricted content.

8. Data Deletion

You can delete your data at any time by:

  • Deleting individual boards and cards within the app
  • Clearing app data in Android Settings
  • Uninstalling the application

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.

10. Your Rights

Since we don't collect personal data, there is no personal information to access, modify, or delete from our systems. All your data is under your control on your device.
